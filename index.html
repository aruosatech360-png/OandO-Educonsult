Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We've got the answers. If you need more support, use our live chat/contact form below!

What are the admission requirements for international students?

Each educational institution may have different admission requirements, but common requirements include proof of English proficiency (such as TOEFL or IELTS scores), transcripts of previous academic work, and financial documents showing ability to pay for tuition and living expenses.

What types of financial aid and scholarships are available?

Various scholarships are available based on merit, need, country of origin, and field of study. We assist in identifying and applying for institutional and external funding opportunities relevant to your profile.

Can international students transfer credits from their home country?

Credit transfer policies vary significantly by institution and country. We guide you through the process of assessing your current qualifications and communicating with the new institution to maximize accepted credits.
